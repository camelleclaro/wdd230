White Space and Clean Design

Apple Inc.

www.apple.com

Apple Inc.'s website implemented a clean design and enough white space. The area between the design elements in every section are very neat. Also, the space within the individual design elements, including the space between the readable characters are neat. Apple's website is minimalists and highlights content one at a time.

Hick's Law

Canva

www.canva.com

Hick's Law implies that the more choices you present your users with, the longer it will take them to come up to a certain decision. Hick's law helps users simplify their decision-making process just like Canva.They simplified the user's decision but at the same time not completely remove it. They arranged their templates and items in a systematic manner to help users reach their desired destination faster.

Fitt's Law

Netflix

www.netflix.com

The design of Netflix's landing page minimizes the user's travel distance with a mouse in selecting an option for there's only one option at the beginning. It reduces time and increases productivity. Netflix doesn't give much option. It's either you sign up or hit the get started button. Just like the Fitt's law itself, the longer the user hit either of the button, the longer he is to explore the site.
